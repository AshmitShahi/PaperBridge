🚀 PaperBridge — AI Research Assistant Platform

PaperBridge is an AI-powered research platform designed to help students and researchers discover, understand, and improve research papers efficiently. It combines semantic search, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and intelligent document analysis to deliver research-backed insights.

📌 Features
🔍 Semantic Research Search
Find research papers using embedding-based search instead of keyword matching
Retrieves highly relevant results using vector similarity (FAISS)

🤖 AI Research Chatbot
Ask questions in natural language
Get clear explanations backed by real research papers
Includes paper references with direct links

📄 Research Paper Reviewer
Upload your own research paper (PDF)
Receive:
Summary
Strengths & weaknesses
Missing elements
Improvement suggestions
Publication readiness score

📚 Multi-Source Integration
arXiv (papers + PDFs)
Semantic Scholar (citations, metadata)
CrossRef (DOI & publication details)
